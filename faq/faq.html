Can anyone sign up for HobbyHarbor?

No, in order to sign up for a HobbyHarbor account, a parent has to enter a school code, pre-determined by the school and verified by the HobbyHarbor team.

How do I control and monitor what my child views?

Many different ways! Alongside a positive, fun experience for your child, our goal is to ensure you trust that they are doing so in a safe, protected way. The HobbyHarbor account you create is a joint account, with PIN-protected settings for you and a colorful space for your child to save their favorite content. Within your settings, you can set a timer for your child that logs them out when finished, set up blocks for various topics and user sharing, and customize a daily report that informs you on all your child’s activities.

Could someone talk to my child without me knowing?

They cannot. We’ve opted to create a space without commenting or messaging, so you don’t have to worry about your child talking to someone they shouldn’t. And because you get a daily report on all their views, creations, and stickers exchanged, you get a full picture of what’s happening during their time on HobbyHarbor.

What kind of topics does HobbyHarbor cover?

While we encourage everyone to share their passions, all of the topics found on HobbyHarbor are focused around learning, whether it be about arts, crafts, animals, science, toys, sports, games, etc. HobbyHarbor is a place for kids to both learn and teach!

What's the purpose of the stickers?

While children are being introduced to the internet, social media, and virtual communities at younger ages than ever before, we wanted to ensure they could interact with a community safely while also having fun. Your child can collect stickers by posting about their favorite activities, give stickers to other users’ content that they like, and receive traded stickers in turn. You do have the option to turn off their ability to trade if you’d like.

How does the recording work when creating a video?

Kids often learn by doing, and HobbyHarbor is a wonderful place for your child to attempt daily challenges, as well as share what they’ve learned and are passionate about. In creating a video to share, they can upload a video they’ve previously recorded or record live from the HobbyHarbor app. There are a set of filters and effects they can apply to add some pizzazz. They’ll name it, add one or more categories to it, and then post it for your approval. Only after you’ve seen and approved it will it be shared with the audience you’ve determined for them.

What if I can't remember my Parental PIN?

Simply click Reset PIN on the PIN screen, and you’re sent an email confirming you want to reset. Follow the prompts to reset.

What's the difference between my profile and my child's?

We’ve created a dual-profile system so that you could could retain as much control as you’d like, while still creating a fun space for your child to save all of their favorites. With your Parental PIN, only you’ll be able to access your side of the profile, though you can peek into their space for a closer look at all that they’ve saved and collected.

How do I access previous Daily Reports?

In your profile, at the bottom of the Settings section, you can click Go in-line with View Daily Report History, which then pulls up a screen that you can filter through according to date and view specific details.
